Our Mission

Why ACS@USC exists

We bring together students who care about cancer prevention, equity, and survivorship. Our mission is to raise funds, awareness, and action so that no one has to face cancer alone.

  • Fund lifesaving cancer research
  • Support patients, survivors, and caregivers
  • Advance cancer justice & health equity
